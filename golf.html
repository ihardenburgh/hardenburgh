 Keeping Trail Elbow Close to Body and Not Too Deep?
Backswing 
• Need to keep trail elbow closer to body in backswing and more forward to prevent cupping of wrists and casting!
• To get this feeling as to how the trail elbow should work in your backswing in a drill, put your lead hand across your chest and under your trail elbow as you take an imaginary club back.
    - Notice how the lead hand prevents your trail elbow from going too deep and becoming disconnected!
